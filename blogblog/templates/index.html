Products

Horizontal Blinds

Available

the most popular window blind designs. They have a traditional look, so consumers are very familiar with them…and very comfortable with them! With horizontal styles, the slats usually measure between one and two inches. They are common choices for windows of small or medium size.

Vertical Blinds

Available

Have bigger slats. These slats hang from rail tracks at the top of the window. Vertical window blinds may be closed or opened via wands or chain pull systems. These styles of blinds are optimal choices for covering bigger windows. They are also ideal for covering sliding glass doors.

Automatic Blinds

Available

Automatic blinds work by using a small motor to gently turn the blind shaft at the touch of a button. This effectively allows the blind fabric to roll up or down a vertical window, or across a sky lantern, to shade or uncover the glazing.
